Established in 1999, study campus started with a handful of students, a few sessions old, the institute scaled new heights of success by providing coaching for Engineering and Medical entrance exam preparation.

Besides the Engineering and Medical domain, study campus brought outstanding results in the International Olympiads as well.
The institute also started providing preparation courses for Study Abroad. Every year, our students get admission into top international universities.
The institute has now become the preferred choice for students, who want to build a strong foundation for their higher studies in Engineering & Medical, or achieve top ranks in competitive exams or Study Abroad programs.
